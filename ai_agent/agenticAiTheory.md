## 1. What is Agentic AI?
Agentic AI is a type of artificial intelligence that can take actions on its own to achieve a goal. Instead of only answering questions, it can use tools and make decisions automatically.

## 2. How is Agentic AI different from a normal chatbot?
A normal chatbot only replies with text. Agentic AI can think, plan, and use tools like APIs to perform real tasks.

## 3. One real-life example of Agentic AI
A smart travel assistant that books flights, checks prices, and sends reminders automatically.
